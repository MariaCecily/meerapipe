Our Story

Established in 2000 with a vision to provide quality and affordable water storage solutions, Meerakunj Private Limited has grown to become a trusted name in the water storage industry. As an ISO 9001:2015 certified company, our principle "Quality is our tradition" guides everything we do. What started as a small manufacturing unit has evolved into a comprehensive solution provider for all water-related needs.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, spans across 21,000 square meters and is equipped with world-class machinery and technology. Located just 12 km from Madurai city, our facility produces a wide range of water storage solutions that cater to residential, commercial, and industrial requirements.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our growth story

2000 - Foundation
The Beginning

Meerakunj was established with a small manufacturing unit focusing on PVC water tanks for residential use.

2005 - Expansion
Product Range Expansion

Expanded our product line to include uPVC plumbing pipes and electrical conduit systems.

2010 - Quality Certification
ISO 9001:2008 Certification

Achieved ISO 9001:2008 certification, reinforcing our commitment to quality management systems.

2015 - Technology Upgrade
Modern Manufacturing

Invested in state-of-the-art manufacturing equipment and expanded our facility to 21,000 sq. meters.

2020 - Market Leadership
Regional Market Leader

Became the leading water storage solution provider in Tamil Nadu with distribution across 15+ states.

2024 - Future Ready
Innovation & Sustainability
