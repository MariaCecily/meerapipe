What We Do

Established in 2000 with a mission to provide quality and affordable water storage solutions, Meerakunj Private Limited has grown to become a trusted name in the industry. As an ISO 9001:2015 certified company, we follow the principle that "Quality is our tradition". Initially starting with manufacturing PVC water tanks, over the years we have expanded our product range to include Water Storage Tanks, Electrical Conduit Pipes & Fittings, and Plumbing solutions. Our manufacturing facility in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, spans across 21,000 square meters, 12 km away from Madurai city, with our headquarters in Madurai, Tamil Nadu.
